Introduction to an Artist

I'm Rachel Donahue at Peep the Moon Studios, and I'm an interdisciplinary artist.
I dabble mostly in painting, but I also enjoy illustration, sculpting, and metalsmithing.

  • Human subjects
  • Animal subjects

Painting

I began painting more than 17 years ago and really enjoy it. I recently began painting portraits of both animal and human subjects.

Sculpting

I have only played around with clay hand modeling, but I would like to try the following in order:

  1. 1. Throwing clay.
  2. 2. Firing in the kiln.
  3. 3. Glazing

Metalsmithing

I enjoy this medium a lot, but it's costly to get started so I haven't done as much here as I would like. Ideally I would love to make large, chunky rings featuring lots of stones and fossils, as well as learning casting techniques.
